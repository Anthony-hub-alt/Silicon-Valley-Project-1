NUTRITION

Introduction

Nutrition is how you keep your body safe and well from diseases and illnesses. Think of your body as a temple, If you do not praise the temple with good food, the temple will get old and rotten. But if you praise the temple with good food the temple will stay up to be very strong. - link for the Australian Health guidelines and healthy eating - https://www.eatforhealth.gov.au/guidelines

Daily Intake for Kids

DAILY INTAKE Your body needs food but you can't just eat 10kg of vegetables at once because then you would get really sick and ill. So there are certain amounts of food you should INTAKE to become very healthy and strong. Australian Guidlines on how much food someone should intake - https://nutritionaustralia.org/fact-sheets/australian-dietary-guidelines-recommended-daily-intakes/

Fitness for Kids

Fitness is where you workout your muscles to help you keep strong and healthy but if you do too much of it you could collapse and hurt yourself badly. So if you want to be healthy and not hurt yourself you should do some exercise per day but not too much. Australian guidelines for fitness and exercise - https://www.health.gov.au/health-topics/physical-activity-and-exercise/physical-activity-and-exercise-guidelines-for-all-australians
